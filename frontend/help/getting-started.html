Uploading Manuscripts

Supported File Formats

We accept the following manuscript formats:

  • .txt - Plain text files (UTF-8 encoding recommended)
  • .pdf - PDF documents (text will be extracted)
  • .docx - Microsoft Word documents (2007 and later)

File Size Limits

  • Maximum file size: 50MB
  • Typical novel: 80,000-100,000 words ‚âà 2-5MB
  • Epic fantasy: 150,000-200,000 words ‚âà 8-12MB
‚ö†Ô∏è Large File Tip: If your manuscript exceeds 50MB, try converting to .txt or compressing images in your .docx file.

Upload Process

  1. Click "Upload New Manuscript" from your dashboard
  2. Select your manuscript file (or drag and drop)
  3. Choose your manuscript's genre from the dropdown
  4. Select a style guide:
    • Chicago Manual of Style: Standard for fiction
    • AP Style: Common for journalism/non-fiction
    • Custom: For specific house styles
  5. Check the copyright attestation checkbox
  6. Click "Upload & Start Analysis"

Copyright Attestation

Before uploading, you must certify that:

  • You are the copyright owner of the manuscript, OR
  • You have obtained all necessary permissions to upload the work
‚ö†Ô∏è Legal Warning: Uploading copyrighted material without permission is illegal and may result in:
  • Immediate account termination
  • Legal action under the DMCA
  • Liability for damages
See our DMCA Policy for details.

What Happens After Upload?

Once you upload a manuscript:

  1. The file is encrypted and stored securely in R2
  2. Analysis jobs are queued for processing
  3. Three AI agents analyze your manuscript in parallel
  4. Progress is displayed in real-time (0-100%)
  5. Typical analysis time: 30-60 seconds
  6. You'll see "Analysis Complete" when finished

Privacy & Security

  • Your manuscripts are encrypted both in transit (HTTPS) and at rest (AES-256)
  • We NEVER use your content to train AI models
  • We NEVER share your manuscripts with third parties
  • You retain full copyright ownership
  • See our Privacy Policy for details
