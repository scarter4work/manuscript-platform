Frequently Asked Questions

Is there really a free plan?
Yes! Our free plan allows you to analyze 1 manuscript per month with full developmental, line, and copy editing analysis. No credit card required to start. You can upgrade anytime to access marketing assets and additional features.
How long does analysis take?
Most manuscripts are analyzed in 30-60 seconds. Our AI agents work in parallel to provide comprehensive feedback across developmental editing, line editing, and copy editing simultaneously. Marketing asset generation takes an additional 30-60 seconds.
What file formats do you support?
We support .txt, .pdf, and .docx files up to 50MB. Your manuscript will be analyzed regardless of formatting, and we'll generate clean EPUB and PDF outputs for Amazon KDP publishing.
Can I cancel my subscription anytime?
Absolutely. You can cancel your subscription at any time from the billing dashboard. You'll continue to have access to your plan features until the end of your current billing period. All your previous analyses remain accessible according to your plan's storage duration.
Is my manuscript secure and private?
Yes. Your manuscripts are encrypted in transit and at rest. We never share your content with third parties or use it to train AI models. You retain full copyright ownership of your work. See our Privacy Policy for details.
Do you offer refunds?
Yes. We offer a 30-day money-back guarantee on all paid plans. If you're not satisfied with our service for any reason, contact us within 30 days of your purchase for a full refund. See our Refund Policy for details.
What makes your AI different from ChatGPT?
Our platform uses specialized AI agents trained specifically for manuscript editing and publishing. Each agent focuses on a specific aspect (developmental, line, copy editing, etc.) and follows professional editing standards. We also integrate directly with Amazon KDP workflows, generating formatted files and marketing assets that ChatGPT can't produce.
Can I use the AI-generated marketing materials commercially?
Yes! All marketing materials, cover designs, and formatted files generated by our platform are yours to use commercially. You have full rights to use them on Amazon KDP, other publishing platforms, your website, and social media.
